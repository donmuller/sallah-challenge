Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremelyexicted about a new cooking technique called sous vide.Insous videcooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temprature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book,Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describe it as "ultra-low-temperature poaching."
